Motorola Radio Toolkit

A simple tool to convert Motorola radio serial numbers to manufacture date. Directly upload Motorola P25 core CSV files and the system will output your CSV with a manufacture date column.

File upload (csv only)

Before uploading a file, ensure that your data is in CSV format and that your serial number column is labeled 'Radio Serial Number' (without quotation marks).
